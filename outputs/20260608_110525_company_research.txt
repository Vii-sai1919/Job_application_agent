**Company Description:**
Google LLC is an American multinational technology corporation focused on information technology, online advertising, search engine technology, email, and software. The company was founded in 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin.

**Core Mission:**
Google's core mission is to organize the world's information and make it universally accessible and useful. The company aims to provide innovative products and services that help people find the information they need and use technology to improve lives around the world.

**Engineering Culture and Values:**
Google has a unique, custom engineering stack, and has built systems differently from competitors since day one, making it a “tech island”. Google's organizational culture gives mental and physical space to employees to do their thing. Besides, Google understands that productivity has to be meaningful – not mindless.

Google's values include:

* **Originality**: Think outside the box
* **Interdependence**: Collaboration is essential
* **Open-mindedness**: Embrace different perspectives
* **Curiosity**: Constant learning and improvement
* **Bias for action**: Take calculated risks

**Current Tech Stack:**
Google uses a combination of open-source and proprietary technologies, including:

* Programming languages: Java, Python, C++, JavaScript
* Web frameworks: React, Angular, Vue.js
* Operating systems: Linux, Android
* Cloud platforms: Google Cloud Platform (GCP), Amazon Web Services (AWS)
* Databases: Bigtable, MySQL
